####  Api查看工具 (集成有stackoverflow等,只支持mac)
[dash](https://kapeli.com/dash)

####  抓包工具
[charles](https://www.charlesproxy.com/)

####  快速游览github项目目录结构 (免费)
[octotree](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/octotree/bkhaagjahfmjljalopjnoealnfndnagc)

####  同步显示操作Android设备屏幕 (超清需付费)
[vysor](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/vysor/gidgenkbbabolejbgbpnhbimgjbffefm)

####  代码片段搜索(支持从 Github、Bitbucket、Google Code、CodePlex、SourceForge 和 Fedora Project 平台搜索公开的源码)
[searchcode](https://searchcode.com/?q=butterknife)

####  源代码在线查看
[grepcode](http://grepcode.com/project/repository.grepcode.com/java/ext/com.google.android/android/)

####  Library搜索
[methodscount](http://www.methodscount.com/)

####  Json格式排版等工具
[json-editor](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/json-editor/lhkmoheomjbkfloacpgllgjcamhihfaj)
